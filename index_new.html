About SNS Financial Services

Our Story

Built to Empower Every Dream

SNS Financial Services was founded with a clear mission: to make financial services accessible, transparent, and empowering for individuals and businesses in Pudukkottai and across Tamil Nadu.

Operating as a proprietorship, we combine the agility of a boutique firm with the credibility of bank-backed partnerships. Our three revenue streams — own-book lending, DSA bank tie-ups, and insurance — allow us to serve a wide range of customer needs under one roof.

1
FY 2022–23

Foundation Year

SNS Financial Services established as a proprietorship in Pudukkottai.

2
FY 2023–24

Growth & Expansion

Expanded team, grew investor base, and launched DSA bank partnerships.

3
FY 2024–25

Portfolio Maturity

Strengthened own-book lending with 7+ investor partners and ₹74L+ portfolio.

4

Presented growth milestones. Target: zero stressed accounts by FY27.
